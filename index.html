Yuho Adam Tsang | Singer & Music Teacher in Toronto

My musical journey began with classical piano at 4 and earned a DipABRSM at just 14 years old. This passion took me from Hong Kong to Vienna, where I initially focused on classical music. By 18, I expanded my interests to Jazz, Pop, and Rock, pursuing Dual Bachelor's Degrees in both Music Production and Vocal Performance in Vienna. In 2025, I completed my Master's Degree in Music Education with an emphasis on Vocal.

My performance experience spans three continents, from symphonic stages to rock band frontman, where music becomes my most vital form of expression. As a NetEase Cloud Music featured creator since 2021, I've released 40+ original songs in Chinese and English, building sonic bridges across cultures.

I have more than 5 years of teaching experience, including private piano and music theory lessons in Hong Kong, vocal and piano instruction in Beijing, and working as a voice teacher in Vienna. These experiences across diverse educational settings and music styles have shaped my teaching philosophy.

My goal is to contribute to a vibrant and supportive musical community, wherever I can be of most value.
